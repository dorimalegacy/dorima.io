how to buy?

Create a wallet for DeFi.
Metamask and Trustwallet are good options.

Fund your wallet with Ethereum by transferring ETH
from centralized exchanges such as Binance.

Connect your wallet, and insert the amount of ETH
you want to purchase $DORIMA for.

Click the gear icon in the upper right corner,
and change slippage to 12%.

Click "Swap", and confirm the transaction
in your wallet provider.
